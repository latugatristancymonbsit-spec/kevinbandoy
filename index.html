Our Story

At Sweet Crumb Bakery, we believe every bite should tell a story. Founded in 2026 by master baker William Castro, our bakery has been serving the community with handcrafted pastries, artisan breads, and custom cakes made from the finest ingredients.

Our secret? Family recipes passed down through generations, combined with modern techniques and a passion for perfection.
